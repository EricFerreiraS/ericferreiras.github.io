# Eric Ferreira dos Santos

Eric Ferreira dos Santos is a Postdoctoral Researcher at Insight Research Ireland Centre for Data Analytics and Dublin City University. His research focuses on explainable artificial intelligence, with particular interest in deep learning interpretability, image classification, knowledge graphs, and knowledge-augmented large language models.

He completed his PhD in Artificial Intelligence at Dublin City University, where his thesis examined deep representations in CNNs, moving from concepts to relations and rules. He also holds an MSc in Computer Science from the Federal University of Rio de Janeiro and a BSc in Computer Science from Veiga de Almeida University.

Before his current postdoctoral role, he worked as a Research Assistant at Insight, a Doctoral Researcher at the SFI Centre for Research Training in AI, and a Visiting Researcher at Imperial College London. His earlier career includes experience in data engineering, business intelligence, system architecture, software development, and team leadership across academic and industry settings.

His research and publications reflect a long-standing interest in making machine learning systems more transparent and trustworthy, especially in domains where interpretation matters. He has also contributed to teaching in computing and information systems, and has been active in the research community through programme committee service and award-supported research.
