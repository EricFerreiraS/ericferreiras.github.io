Business Intelligence is a module designed to introduce students to the principles, methods, and applications of data-driven decision-making in organisational settings. 
The module examines how business intelligence supports the collection, integration, analysis, and presentation of information for managerial and strategic purposes, 
with particular attention to the role of data in improving performance, planning, and operational insight.

The teaching emphasises both conceptual understanding and practical application. Students are introduced to core topics such as data sourcing, data quality, reporting, visualisation, dashboards, 
and the transformation of raw data into meaningful business knowledge. The module also considers the relationship between business intelligence, information systems, and organisational decision processes,
enabling students to critically assess how analytical approaches can be used to support evidence-based practice.

The module provides a foundation for further study in data analytics, management information systems, and related areas of digital business transformation.
