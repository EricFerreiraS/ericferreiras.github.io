Cyber Security is a comprehensive diploma programme that introduces students to the fundamental principles, tools, and practices used to protect digital systems, networks, and information assets. The course develops an understanding of how cyber threats arise, how organisations can identify vulnerabilities, and how appropriate controls, policies, and response strategies can be implemented to reduce risk and strengthen resilience.

The programme covers a broad range of topics, including information security fundamentals, networking basics, secure communications, browser and software security, cybercrime, cyber law, compliance, intellectual property, and professional ethics. It also addresses risk assessment, incident preparation and response, access control, business continuity, and the management of vulnerabilities and security controls.

In addition to technical and procedural aspects, the course places strong emphasis on governance, legal responsibilities, and practical decision-making in the context of digital security.
