Information and Communication Technology is a foundational module designed to develop students’ understanding of core digital tools and their role in contemporary business practice. The module introduces learners to the effective use of information and communication technologies to support organisational tasks, improve productivity, and enable professional collaboration in digitally mediated environments.

The module places emphasis on the practical application of ICT in business settings, including digital communication, document preparation, information handling, and the use of technology to support day-to-day operations. Students are encouraged to build confidence in working with digital systems and to understand how ICT contributes to efficiency, flexibility, and effective workplace interaction.

In addition to technical skills, the module also develops students’ awareness of the wider role of ICT in business and society. It supports the development of transferable digital competencies that are increasingly required across sectors, preparing learners for further study and for participation in technology-enabled professional environments.
